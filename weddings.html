Ceremony Options
Find the ceremony that's right for you

I welcome and celebrate all couples who are in love and want to get married.

Personalized Wedding Ceremony
(most requested by couples)

Best for: Couples who want a fully custom ceremony that reflects their unique love story.

We’ll work closely together to create a ceremony written specifically for you — from tone and structure to personal details and meaningful moments.

What’s included:

  • Multiple meetings (Zoom or in person) to get to know you and plan your ceremony
  • A fully personalized ceremony written just for you
  • Help with writing personal vows (if requested)
  • Inclusion of meaningful stories and special elements
  • Officiating your wedding ceremony
  • Mailing your marriage licence to the Registrar General of Ontario within 48 hours
  • Portable sound system and headset microphone, if required

Fee: $500

Rehearsal (optional): $150 (if requested & subject to availability)

Travel: 20 km included from Officiant’s home in Burlington, then $0.60/km

Standard Ceremonies
(Elopements, Vow Renewals & Simple Weddings)

Best for: Couples wanting a meaningful, structured ceremony with simplicity and choice

You’ll choose from two thoughtfully prepared ceremony options, allowing you to select the tone and style that best suits your celebration.

What’s included:

  • Initial meeting to get to know you and discuss your ceremony
  • Follow-up meeting closer to your wedding date to confirm details
  • Choice of two prepared ceremonies
  • Officiating your wedding ceremony
  • Mailing your marriage licence to the Registrar General of Ontario within 48 hours
  • Portable sound system and headset microphone, if required

Fee: $400

Rehearsal (optional): $150 (if requested & subject to availability)

Travel: 20 km included from Officiant’s home in Burlington, then $0.60/km

Simple Legal Ceremony

Best for: Couples who want a simple, straightforward legal marriage ceremony in Ontario

A respectful and meaningful legal ceremony with minimal planning and a focus on the essentials.

What’s included:

  • One planning meeting to confirm ceremony details
  • A prepared legal ceremony
  • Officiating your wedding ceremony
  • Mailing your marriage licence to the Registrar General of Ontario within 48 hours

Fee: $300

Travel: 20 km included from Officiant’s home in Burlington, then $0.60/km

Not sure which option is right for you?

I’m happy to help guide you toward the ceremony that feels like the best fit for your day.
