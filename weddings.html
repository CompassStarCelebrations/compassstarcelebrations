Ceremony Options
Find the ceremony that's right for you

I welcome and celebrate all couples who are in love and want to get married.

Personalized Wedding Ceremony
(most requested by couples)

Best for: Couples who want a fully custom ceremony that reflects their unique love story.

We'll work closely together to create a ceremony written specifically for you, from tone and structure to personal details and meaningful moments.

Includes: personalized planning meetings, custom-written ceremony, vow support (if requested), officiating, and filing your marriage licence with Ontario within 48 hours.

Fee: $500. Rehearsal (optional): $150. Travel: 20 km included from Burlington, then $0.60/km.

Standard Ceremonies
(Elopements, Vow Renewals & Simple Weddings)

Best for: Couples wanting a meaningful, structured ceremony with simplicity and choice.

You'll choose from prepared ceremony options, with meetings to confirm tone, style, and details for your day.

Includes: planning meetings, choice of prepared ceremonies, officiating, marriage licence filing, and portable sound system if required.

Fee: $400. Rehearsal (optional): $150. Travel: 20 km included from Burlington, then $0.60/km.

Simple Legal Ceremony

Best for: Couples who want a simple, straightforward legal marriage ceremony in Ontario.

A respectful legal ceremony with minimal planning and focus on the essentials.

Includes one planning meeting, a prepared legal ceremony, officiating, and marriage licence filing.

Fee: $300. Travel: 20 km included from Burlington, then $0.60/km.

Not sure which option is right for you? I'm happy to help guide you toward the ceremony that feels like the best fit for your day. Sharing life's journeys together!
