Let's Celebrate!

Whether you are celebrating a wedding, elopement, vow renewal, or welcoming a child through birth or adoption, as a licensed Humanist Officiant in Ontario I would be honoured to help create a ceremony that reflects your story and the moment you are celebrating.

Sharing life's journeys together!

Your Wedding, Your Way!

Every couple’s story is unique, and your ceremony should feel that way too. Whether you’re planning a grand celebration, a quiet elopement, or something beautifully simple, I’ll work with you to create a ceremony that feels personal, meaningful, and memorable.

So Many Celebrations!

Have you been married for 5 years or 50? Celebrate your love once again with a heartfelt vow renewal! This is a beautiful opportunity to gather your family and dear friends as you reaffirm your commitment to one another.

Welcoming a new little one or a newly adopted child into your family? What a wonderful occasion to surround them with love! Together, we create a meaningful celebration that honours your growing family and the journey ahead.

Let me help you make these special moments truly unforgettable.

It would be an honour to celebrate with you and be part of

such an important moment in your lives.

Your ceremony should feel warm, welcoming, inclusive and uniquely yours.

Together we will create a celebration filled with meaning, joy, and lasting memories.
