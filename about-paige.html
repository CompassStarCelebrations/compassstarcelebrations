Paige Bennett

Licensed Wedding Officiant in Ontario

Throughout my life, I have followed many pathways — growing up in both northern and southern Ontario, travelling when possible, and building a career centred around working with and supporting people.

My career in education included roles as a teacher, vice principal, and special education specialist, as well as teaching at the university level in Faculties of Education.

Community involvement has also always been important to me, including the St. John Ambulance Therapy Dog Program, where I volunteer with my golden retriever, Buck.

When I’m not officiating ceremonies, you’ll find me spending time with family and friends or enjoying walks with Buck.

I became an Officiant because I wanted to be part of life’s meaningful moments and celebrations. It is truly an honour and privilege to help create ceremonies that reflect each couple’s story, values, and journey together. I look forward to being part of this chapter in your life!
